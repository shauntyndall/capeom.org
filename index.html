2022 Odyssey of the Mind World Finals

The Cape Henlopen School District is proud to announce that four of our local elementary school teams (Milton ES and Rehoboth ES) have advanced to the Odyssey of the Mind World Finals competition. The competition will take place May 25th-29th in Ames, Iowa. Our students are thrilled to represent the Cape Region at this prestigious event.

Odyssey of the Mind (OM) teaches students to develop and use their natural creativity to become problem-solvers. Students are faced with a problem in need of an original solution and then present their solution to judges. These skills can then be applied to real life and are the exact skills business owners are seeking in todayâ€™s graduates. OM emphasizes teamwork, budgeting, time management, public speaking and so much more.

Today our Milton and Rehoboth Elementary teams are seeking to raise $30,000 to assure all students can attend World Finals. Funds will help cover room and board as well as airline tickets. The cost per student is over $1,500. All funds raised will be split evenly between the four teams.

Please support this opportunity for our students with a pledge amount below. You will be helping to make a difference in our local community. All funds will need to be received by April 25th.
